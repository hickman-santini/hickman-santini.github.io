I'm a data scientist and effective altruist. I hold a Masters in Computer Science from Virginia Tech, where I was advised by Benjamin Jantzen and Shaddi Hasan. I'm currently at the Forecasting Research Institute. Formerly I worked at nLine, Inc., a startup that measures power quality and reliability at outlets in homes, businesses and healthcare facilities around the world, seeking to improve the reach and resilience of energy infrastructure. Before that I worked at the MITRE Corporation, doing test & evaluation for crowdsourced intelligence projects.
